How to play

1 Click on a category you would like to play.

2 In each category 10 questions will be selected randonly from the question bank.

3 Each correct answer will highlight green and each incorrect answer will highlight red.

4 Every correct answer is worth 10 points. There is no negative marking.

5 You may leave the game anytime by pressing the exit button.
